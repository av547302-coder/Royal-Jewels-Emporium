About Royal Jewels Emporium

Located in the heart of Johri Bazar, Jaipur, Royal Jewels Emporium is known for handcrafted bridal, Polki, Kundan, diamond and gold jewellery with timeless designs and unmatched craftsmanship.

From intimate weddings to grand royal celebrations, we design pieces that tell your story and become family heirlooms for generations.
